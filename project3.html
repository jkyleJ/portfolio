Branded Motion Graphics Package

Role

  • Motion Graphic Designer

Project Type

  • Promotional

Tools Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Audition

Team

  • Jeongwon Jin - Designer

Duration

  • Sep 2023 - Oct 2024

In this motion graphics project, my task is to create a branded motion graphics package that includes a logo reveal animation, lower thirds, and a transition bumper. These assets will be provided as editable MoGRT (Motion Graphics Template) files, allowing for easy customization and future use. These assets will be showcased in a branded video I will develop.

The subsequent phase involved the creation of dynamic motion graphics, starting with a captivating logo reveal animation designed to effectively represent the brand. Employing various motion graphics techniques, the logo was brought to life, leaving a memorable impression in harmony with the brand's aesthetic and values. Additionally, visually appealing lower thirds templates were crafted to elegantly display text information on footage, ensuring alignment with the brand's visual style and prioritizing readability.

A visually engaging transition bumper was developed, seamlessly transitioning between scenes while incorporating brand elements for consistency. These elements, including the logo reveal, lower thirds, and transition bumper, were then converted into editable MoGRT files, organized for ease of use and customization in future projects. To showcase the motion graphics package, a branded video was created using relevant stock footage or original captures, edited using a combination of Adobe Premiere and After Effects. Evaluation focused on factors such as visual impact, brand consistency, MoGRT file usability, and the output of the final demo reel, with a comprehensive presentation covering creative choices, the design process, and the rationale behind branding decisions.
