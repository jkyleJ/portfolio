Create motion graphic works such as animated logos, animated banners, and motion posters. Cooperate with other creative Humber E-Sport creative team members for a mobile platform such as Tictok, Instagram, or YouTube shorts and stream their match.

The next step involved crafting dynamic motion graphics with graphic assets that our team made together. Initially designed in Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop underwent a meticulous process before being imported into Adobe After Effects. Here, I orchestrated the convergence of our graphic assets with motion graphic design, infusing life and movement into the elements to enhance their visual impact and engagement. As the stages reached their culmination, the transition to Adobe After Effects marked a pivotal phase.

My focus was on harmonizing and unifying the pace, ensuring a seamless interplay between meticulously curated visual elements. This involved a deliberate effort to synchronize rhythm and timing, aiming to craft a cohesive and immersive experience for the audience. These motion graphics were created for various purposes, including animated logos, banners, and posters, collaborating with the Humber E-Sport creative team for platforms like TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube shorts, showcasing their matches through engaging streams.
