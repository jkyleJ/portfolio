Branded Motion Graphics Promotional Campaign

Role

  • Motion Graphic Designer

Project Type

  • Branding

Tools Used

  • Adobe After Effects
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Audition

Team

  • Jeongwon Jin - Designer

Duration

  • Nov 2023 - Dec 2024

The objective of this project is to showcase my creativity and design skills by creating a promotional campaign for a travel company named Agoda. The campaign will be designed for a specific media platform, including social media and digital signage.

Review the selected brand's values and target audience, and explore existing promotional material. Consider the selected platform's format and specifications to optimize my campaign for maximum impact. Based on my research, develop a plan of action where I will gain the most traction for my target audience. Create a storyboard outlining the visual flow and key scenes of my motion graphics campaign, and then, using my storyboards, develop an animatic to demonstrate my ideas and the flow of my assets.

Utilize a combination of Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator, and Procreate to design and animate your campaign and create visually appealing graphics, typography, and transitions to captivate the audience. Finally, export my campaign in a suitable format for the platform, considering factors such as aspect ratio, file size, and compatibility.
